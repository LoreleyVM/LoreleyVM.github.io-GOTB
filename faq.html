FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upload my song to streaming sites with purchase of a lease?

Yes, you can upload your song to all streaming services online (Spotify,Apple Music, etc)

When do I get my files after purchase?

Files are downloadable instantly after purchase from your receipt screen. They are also emailed to the purchase email you entered when chekcking out.

What happens if I go over the stream limit?

Each license has a finite stream limit (besides the unlimited). If your track looks like it’s going to go over, simply contact me at contraofuture@gmail.com for an upgrade request and you will only have to pay the difference to upgrade (past purchase will be credited)
